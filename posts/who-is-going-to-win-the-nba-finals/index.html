Who will win the NBA Finals?

NBA
basketball
playoffs
Author

Nick Evans

Published

May 5, 2023

The NBA regular season no longer translates to the postseason. This year four of the top seven best records in the league lost in the first round this year. You would think winning 58 games like the Milwaukee Bucks did would result in a championship or at least an appearance. Well, that would be wrong as the 8th seed Miami Heat beat them in five games which is unheard of in the past NBA. In fact, it is so unheard of that the Milwaukee Bucks odds to win this series were -1200 and that was the 4th largest odds to be favored ever and lose the series. There are many reasons for why the NBA regular season is different than it ever has been and why nothing you do in the regular season guarantees success. Two of the main reasons are load management to prevent health and how the league is so more three oriented than it ever was before. This leads to volatile shooting nights that result in unpredictable results. If a team is hitting their threes for a four-game stretch that they never usually do that team can win the series even if they aren’t the better team. With all this being said let’s look at the final eight teams and see who has the best shot at a title.

Looking at offensive and defensive ratings for the whole season three to four teams catch my eye. The New York Knicks have the best defensive rating left in the playoffs while also having one of the more efficient offenses in the league, congrats New York. What I see next is the Philadelphia 76ers, Boston Celtics and Denver Nuggets have some of best offenses in the league but lack slightly with their defensive play, but they aren’t bad except the Celtics. This chart did show me some things about these teams that I didn’t know before but let’s really say what will happen. The Knicks are a five seed and should be happy getting to the eastern conference finals but most likely will lose before then. The Celtics defense appears to be one of the worst in the league and it will lose them a series before getting to the finals. Then there is two teams left, the Nuggets and the 76ers as real contenders.

I’ve been saying it throughout this whole article that the NBA is different than it was and what do you need to do to win an NBA game today? Make threes and get to the line. Looking at the 76ers they are one of the best teams in the league at both shooting 39% from three this season while also getting to the line 25 times a game. The Nuggets shoot 38% from three and they also add 22 free throw attempts a game. If these two teams can keep this pace up the rest of the playoffs, I believe they both have a good shot at making the finals. I’m not a betting man but if I was, I believe I would bet the Nuggets to win the NBA Finals.
